Frequently asked questions
Is Codex PREP the official OOU Post-UTME portal?

No — Codex PREP is an independent practice platform to help you prepare. Your actual Post-UTME registration and screening happen through OOU's official admissions portal.

How many free attempts do I get?

Free accounts get a limited number of CBT attempts across all subjects. Once used, you'll need to upgrade to Regular or VIP for unlimited practice — check the Pricing page for current plans.

Are the questions real past questions?

Most subjects use compiled past questions with answers verified against reliable sources. Where a question bank includes AI-assisted content, we aim to note that clearly so you know what you're studying.

Does meeting the JAMB cut-off guarantee admission?

No. Meeting the minimum JAMB score only qualifies you to be invited for Post-UTME screening. Final admission also depends on your Post-UTME score, department capacity, and that year's competition — none of which are published in advance.

Can I use this on my phone?

Yes — Codex PREP is built mobile-first and works on any modern phone browser.

I forgot my password — what do I do?

Go to the login page and tap "Forgot password?" — you'll get a reset link by email. If you signed up with Google, just use the Google sign-in button instead.

How do I request a refund or report a payment issue?

Reach out directly via call or WhatsApp above with your registered email and payment reference, and we'll sort it out.
